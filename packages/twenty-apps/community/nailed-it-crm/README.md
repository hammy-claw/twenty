# Nailed It CRM

This Twenty app is the first serious pass at replacing Houzz for Nailed It Developments.

## What it covers

- lead intake and qualification
- project handoff
- estimates and approvals
- selections and allowance tracking
- change orders and scope discipline
- production planning at a high level
- vendor directory and trade tracking
- AI-assisted summaries, follow-ups, and scope checks

## Working rules

- Premium, simple, and low-friction
- Phone-first qualification before consultation
- Keep procurement separate from production planning
- Anything not explicitly approved becomes a change order
- AI should help the team move faster, not add noise
